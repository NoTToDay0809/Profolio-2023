Title: “Mr. Still-Life”

Year: 2023 Mister Still Life represents a human being who is constantly under social control. Some of us, despite having wealth or possessions, feel like a wooden mannequin or robot. Fruits represent excess, pleasure, overeating, and temptation. I selected this image because to its composition, which highlights the distance between each subject and uses contrast lighting to highlight the subject. To draw attention, the colour highlighted or contrasted the negatives. NoT.ToDay by Lee Teck Shen
